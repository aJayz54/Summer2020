East Bay Academics

Hello! We are a group of alumni from The College Preparatory School. The aim of these classes are to help students feel more confident in their skills and abilities in preparation for the coming school year. We aim to provide a dedicated and specialized educational experience for students in both classroom and 1 on 1 settings. We have experience tutoring a wide range of subjects and have worked with a wide range of students.

We're a group of students who attend many of the top public, private, and liberal arts colleges and universities, such as UPenn, UMich, and Swarthmore! We are very confident that the preparations we have made towards this educational endeavor in addition to the education we have received allows us to give high quality education without overcharging.

We hope you choose us to help you and your children feel more confident going into this coming and future academic years! If you have any questions please refer to our email.
